In the quiet hum of a midnight screen,
Where thoughts glow brighter than they seem,
I trace the lines of what could be,
Between the code and memory.

A world unfolds in silent light,
Built from dreams that chase the night,
Every pixel holds a spark,
A constellation in the dark.

The clock forgets to count the hours,
While logic blooms like hidden flowers,
And somewhere deep in circuits wide,
A restless heart learns how to guide.

Not all journeys leave a trace,
Some exist in unseen space,
Where questions echo, soft and low,
And answers take their time to grow.

I’ve walked through errors, red and bold,
Through stories half-written, never told,
Yet every flaw became a key,
Unlocking newer clarity.

The silence speaks when noise is gone,
A gentle push to carry on,
Through every doubt, through every fall,
There’s something waiting past it all.

We build, we break, we start again,
Like wandering code in loops of then,
But every cycle, every try,
Brings us closer to the “why.”

A whisper in the lines we write,
A hidden truth in black and white,
That meaning isn’t always found,
But slowly grows from common ground.

So let the errors come and stay,
They light a different kind of way,
For in the chaos, raw and true,
You find the path that’s meant for you.

And when the final line is drawn,
And all illusions seem withdrawn,
You’ll see it wasn’t just the end—
But every step that chose to bend.

The night fades softly into blue,
A quiet promise born anew,
That every dream you dare to chase,
Will find, in time, its rightful place.
